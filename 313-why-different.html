Why We're Different

Most podcast agencies will tell you they "care more" or that they're "data-driven." Here's what actually sets 313 apart.

We've Been in the Chair

The Dillon England Show has generated 1.5M+ organic YouTube views. We don't just produce podcasts—we host one. We've booked guests, recorded episodes, dealt with technical failures, and learned what actually works.

We've experienced how costly mistakes can be. Now we're passionate about helping you avoid them and succeed faster than we did.

YouTube-First Strategy

YouTube is owned by Google. It's the #2 search engine on the planet. Your prospects are already there—searching for solutions, vetting credibility before they take a meeting.

We position your podcast where customers are already looking, not where we hope they'll find you.

We Track What Matters

Views and downloads build your authority and reach. That's important—your show should be visible where prospects search. But visibility alone doesn't drive business. We track both: your exposure metrics AND guest-to-client conversions, referrals generated, and pipeline built.

21%* of guests from The Dillon England Show resulted in business opportunities—either becoming clients or generating referrals. We don't celebrate download numbers—we celebrate deals closed and relationships built. That's the difference between a podcast and a business development tool.

A Proven System

We've built a proprietary system for tracking guests from conversation to client. We know which guests are worth having, how to position them, how to follow up, and how to convert.

You get a process proven by experience, not guesswork.
