Common Questions

We get these questions a lot. Here's what we tell people.

If podcasting works this well, why doesn't everyone do it?

+

Most people focus on the wrong metrics. They nail the visibility part—they get views, downloads, exposure. Great. But then they don't have a strategy to convert those viewers into actual relationships and revenue. They quit before momentum builds because they're not tracking what matters. A podcast without a conversion strategy is just expensive content creation.

A podcast IS powerful. It builds authority, reach, and credibility. But only if you combine it with a real strategy for turning listeners into clients. Most agencies stop at the visibility piece. We keep going.

Even if I'm convinced, how do I actually execute this?

+

That's where we come in. You might know a podcast is powerful, but the execution is overwhelming. We handle guest strategy, outreach, recording, editing, distribution, and conversion tracking. You show up for conversations. We handle everything else.

Will this take a ton of my time?

+

No. You show up for the conversations. That's it. Everything else—strategy, production, editing, distribution, tracking—we handle. Your job is to be good at talking to people. We handle everything else.

How long before we see results?

+

That depends on what "results" means to you. Are you looking for lead generation—guests converting into actual clients and revenue? Or exposure and authority building? Those have different timelines.

Many of our clients see real momentum within 3-6 months. You'll have guests booked, episodes live, content flowing, and the first indicators that the system is working. Lead generation takes a bit longer—relationships compound over time. But by month 6, most clients are seeing meaningful traction from guest-to-lead conversions.

That's why we have minimums. A podcast isn't a quick win. It's a relationship engine. You need time for it to work.

What if we already have a podcast but it's not working?

+

That's our specialty. Most broken podcasts lack strategy or tracking. We audit what's happening, rebuild your strategy, and implement the system that actually works. Start with a Podcast Audit and we'll show you what to fix.
